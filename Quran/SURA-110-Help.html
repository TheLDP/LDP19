SURA CX
HELP

    MEDINA .–3 Verses

    In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful

  1. WHEN the HELP of God and the victory arrive,
  2. And thou seest men entering the religion of God by troops;
  3. Then utter the praise of thy Lord, implore His pardon; for He loveth to turn in mercy.
