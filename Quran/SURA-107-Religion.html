SURA CVII
RELIGION

    MECCA .–7 Verses

    In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful

  1. WHAT thinkest thou of him who treateth our RELIGION as a lie?
  2. He it is who trusteth away the orphan,
  3. And stirreth not others up to feed the poor.
  4. Woe to those who pray,
  5. But in their prayer are careless;
  6. Who make a shew of devotion,
  7. But refuse help to the needy.
