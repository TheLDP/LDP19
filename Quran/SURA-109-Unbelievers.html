SURA CIX
UNBELIEVERS

    MECCA .–6 Verses

    In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful

  1. SAY: O ye UNBELIEVERS!
  2. I worship not that which ye worship,
  3. And ye do not worship that which I worship;
  4. I shall never worship that which ye worship,
  5. Neither will ye worship that which I worship.
  6. To you be your religion; to me my religion.
