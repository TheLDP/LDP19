SURA CIV
THE BACKBITER

    MECCA .–9 Verses

    In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful

  1. Woe to every BACKBITER, Defamer!
  2. Who amasseth wealth and storeth it against the future!
  3. He thinketh surely that his wealth shall be with him for ever.
  4. Nay! for verily he shall be flung into the Crushing Fire;
  5. And who shall teach thee what the Crushing Fire is?
  6. It is God's kindled fire,
  7. Which shall mount above the hearts of the damned;
  8. It shall verily rise over them like a vault,
  9. On outstretched columns.
