SURA XCVII
POWER

    MECCA .–5 Verses

    In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful

  1. VERILY, we have caused It to descend on the night of POWER.
  2. And who shall teach thee what the night of power is?
  3. The night of power excelleth a thousand months:
  4. Therein descend the angels and the spirit by permission of their Lord for every matter;
  5. And all is peace till the breaking of the morn.
