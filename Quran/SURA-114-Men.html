SURA CXIV
MEN

    MECCA OR MEDINA.–6 Verses

    In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful

  1. SAY: I betake me for refuge to the Lord of MEN,
  2. The King of men,
  3. The God of men,
  4. Against the mischief of the stealthily withdrawing whisperer,
  5. Who whispereth in man's breast–
  6. Against djinn and men.
