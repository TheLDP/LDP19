SURA LXXXVI. THE NIGHT-COMER

    MECCA .–17 Verses

    In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful

  1. BY the heaven, and by the NIGHT-COMER!
  2. But who shall teach thee what the night-comer is?
  3. 'Tis the star of piercing radiance.
  4. Over every soul is set a guardian.
  5. Let man then reflect out of what he was created.
  6. He was created of the poured-forth germs,
  7. Which issue from the loins and breastbones:
  8. Well able then is God to restore him to life,–
  9. On the day when all secrets shall be searched out,
  10. And he shall have no other might or helper.
  11. I swear by the heaven which accomplisheth its cycle,
  12. And by the earth which openeth her bosom,
  13. That this Koran is a discriminating discourse,
  14. And that it is not frivolous.
  15. They plot a plot against thee,
  16. And I will plot a plot against them.
  17. Deal calmly therefore with the infidels; leave them awhile alone.
