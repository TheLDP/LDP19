SURA CXIII
THE DAYBREAK

    MECCA OR MEDINA.–5 Verses

    In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful

  1. SAY: I betake me for refuge to the Lord of the DAY BREAK
  2. Against the mischiefs of his creation;
  3. And against the mischief of the night when it overtaketh me;
  4. And against the mischief of weird women;
  5. And against the mischief of the envier when he envieth.
