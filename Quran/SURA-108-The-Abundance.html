SURA CVIII
THE ABUNDANCE

    MECCA .–3 Verses

    In the Name of God, the Compassionate, the Merciful

  1. TRULY we have given thee an ABUNDANCE;
  2. Pray therefore to the Lord, and slay the victims.
  3. Verily whoso hateth thee shall be childless.
